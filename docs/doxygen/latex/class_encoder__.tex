\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The encoder class is used to control a classic multiturn rotary encoder with click button

The encoder is able to sense a left or right turn thanks to two pins connected to on different part of the wheel. The wheel turning pull-\/up or down the pins in different order.

The alternate L\+OW and H\+I\+GH state of the pins describes two squared waves 90 degrees out of phase.
